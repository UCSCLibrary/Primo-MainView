 Where's Cruzcat?

This Search replaces Cruzcat and allows researchers to search the content previously available in Cruzcat alongside articles and other content available through our subscription databases and open access resources. Look for options to limit your search to books and other resource types on the search results page.

 How does this compare to Melvyl?

Melvyl is great for finding books and materials held by other libraries in the University of California and across the country. In contrast, this Search tool includes articles and other online resources not available in Melvyl and provides quicker access to full text for UCSC researchers.
